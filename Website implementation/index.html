Featured Project

The Downsizers

Denise Espinoza's architectural project for DAB201 Assessment 2 - Residential Architecture B is titled "The Downsizers: Ageing in Place," and focuses on the trend of downsizing residential homes for ageing Australians. Inspired by the Eames House in California, the project analyzes the structure, circulation, private/public spaces, materiality, and relationship with the site of the Eames House, Frank House, and Twin Houses in Brisbane. The project aims to design a home that benefits downsizers by considering physical and financial aspects of downsizing and analyzing the site's local and state heritage, nearby resources, and environmental factors such as wind direction and noise. The project also requires the student to create a plan based on the research.
